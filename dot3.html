Epson LQ-310

The Epson LQ-310 is a 24-pin dot matrix printer that was released in 2009. It is a narrow carriage printer, which means that it can only print on paper up to 10 inches wide. The LQ-310 has a print speed of up to 416 characters per second (cps) in draft mode and 103 cps in letter quality mode. It also has a long lifespan, with a mean time between failures (MTBF) of 10,000 hours.


SPECs

  • 24-pin dot matrix printer
  • Narrow carriage (10 inches)
  • Print speed of up to 416 cps in draft mode and 103 cps in letter quality mode
  • Long lifespan (MTBF of 10,000 hours)
  • USB, parallel, and serial connectivity
  • Black ribbon cartridge that yields up to 2.5 million characters
