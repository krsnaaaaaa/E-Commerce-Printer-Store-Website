Epson FX-2175II

The Epson FX-2175II is a high-performance, 9-pin dot matrix printer that is designed for demanding business applications. It offers fast print speeds of up to 730 characters per second (cps) in draft mode and 480 cps in high-quality mode. It can also handle up to six-part forms, making it ideal for printing invoices, receipts, and other multi-page documents.

In terms of connectivity, the FX-2175II has USB 2.0 and parallel ports, so you can connect it to most computers. It also has an optional network interface, so you can share it with multiple users on a network.


SPECs

  • Print speeds of up to 730 cps in draft mode and 480 cps in high-quality mode
  • Handles up to six-part forms
  • Mean time between failures (MTBF) of 15,000 power on hours
  • USB 2.0 and parallel ports
  • Optional network interface
  • One-year warranty
