Epson LQ-1310

The Epson LQ-1310 is a 24-pin dot matrix printer that was released in 1994. It is a wide carriage printer that can print up to 132 columns of text in draft mode and 66 columns in letter quality mode. It has a print speed of up to 416 characters per second (cps) in draft mode and 60 cps in letter quality mode.!

The LQ-1310 has a built-in tractor feed and can handle cut sheet paper, envelopes, and labels. It also has a parallel port and a serial port for connecting to a computer.
The LQ-1310 is a reliable and durable printer that is well-suited for businesses that need to print large volumes of text. It is also a good choice for home users who need a printer that can handle a variety of printing tasks.


SPECs

  • 24-pin dot matrix printer
  • Wide carriage (132 columns)
  • Print speed of up to 416 cps (draft mode) and 60 cps (letter quality mode)
  • Built-in tractor feed
  • Handles cut sheet paper, envelopes, and labels
  • Parallel and serial port
