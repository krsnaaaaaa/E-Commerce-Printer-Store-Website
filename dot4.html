Epson FX-890II

The Epson FX-890II is a high-performance 9-pin dot matrix printer that is built for demanding print environments. It is known for its durability, reliability, and speed. It has a long life cycle and can withstand dust, humidity, and extreme temperatures. It also has a 3-year limited warranty.

The FX-890II can print at speeds of up to 738 characters per second (cps) in draft mode and 115 cps in near letter quality (NLQ) mode. It can also print on a variety of media, including paper, envelopes, and cardboard.
The FX-890II is compatible with a variety of operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. It also supports a variety of printer languages, including Epson ESC/P, IBM PPDS, and MICROLINE.
