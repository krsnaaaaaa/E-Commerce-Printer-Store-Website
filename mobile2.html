HPRT MT810

The HPRT MT810 is a portable mini thermal printer that is perfect for on-the-go printing. It is small and lightweight, making it easy to take with you wherever you go. It can be connected to your phone or tablet via Bluetooth, so you can easily print photos, documents, and receipts. It also has a long battery life and quick printing speed. However, it is important to note that it only prints in black and white and is not as fast as some other portable printers.


SPECs

  • Compact and portable
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • High-resolution printing
  • Long battery life
  • Quick printing speed
  • Easy to use
